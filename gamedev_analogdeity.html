Analog Deity

Swipe sideways to see more images.

Compare images of people/scenes and deduce which one is the real image and which one is a ‘deity’. Guess wrong and you will lose lives, guess right and you will be rewarded accordingly. This game has been made within Unity 6 and is built for Android devices. It contains examples of micro-transactions and conventional mobile game mechanics.

Unity version: 6000.0.23f1 LTS

Target platform: Android
